ARTICLE 21: Essex Ag-Tech assessment

Quick facts
- Category: Finance
- Sponsor: Select Board
- Vote threshold: Majority
- Key: $749,920 from taxation
- Stakes: low
- Recommendation: favorable (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Marblehead is a member of the Essex North Shore Agricultural and Technical School District, a regional vocational high school. Each member town pays an annual assessment based on enrollment and a weighted vote of the district school committee. Marblehead’s FY27 share is $749,920, set by the district and not negotiable locally.
- 
- No new vote was taken at the April 27 hearing because the assessment is set and FinCom had already voted favorable. The April 27 discussion was informational, focused on the new state vocational lottery, enrollment risk and possible future budget exposure tied to changes in how vocational seats are allocated.

ARTICLE 19: Fire union contract

Quick facts
- Category: Compensation
- Sponsor: Select Board
- Vote threshold: Majority
- Key: Three-year deal: 3% / 3% / 3.5%
- Stakes: medium
- Recommendation: favorable (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- When the town reaches a collective bargaining agreement with a union, Town Meeting must vote to fund it. The fire contract with IAFF Local 2043 is now finalized — a three-year agreement with raises of 3 percent, 3 percent and 3.5 percent.
- 
- The FY27 budget already assumes a 3 percent cost-of-living adjustment, and officials said no separate appropriation is being requested for year one. At its April 27 hearing, FinCom voted to recommend that Town Meeting ratify the contract.

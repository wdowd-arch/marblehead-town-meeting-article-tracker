We believe that an override is necessary at this time to maintain the level of services residents receive 
today. The Finance Committee voted unanimously to recommend the passing of Article 29, which would 
move the override request to the ballot to be voted on by residents at the June election/vote. 
 
On April 10, 2026, S&P Global affirmed Marblehead’s AAA rating; however, the outlook was revised 
from ‘stable’ to ‘negative’ due to budgetary stress. At the start of FY27, the Town's reserve accounts will 
constitute approximately 2% of the operating budget. Town officials remain committed to the objective of 
building reserves to 5% of the operating budget moving forward. It is crucial for the Town to sustain a 
sufficient level of reserves to preserve its AAA bond rating each year.

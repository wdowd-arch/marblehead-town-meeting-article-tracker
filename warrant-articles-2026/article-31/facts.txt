ARTICLE 31: Admin benefit amendments

Quick facts
- Category: Compensation
- Sponsor: Select Board
- Vote threshold: Majority
- Key: Three personal days, +$750 longevity
- Stakes: medium
- Recommendation: favorable (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Would enhance the benefits package for non-union administrative employees to bring it closer to what collective bargaining units receive. Proposed changes:
-  Three personal days
-  $750 longevity increase
-  Accelerated timeline for earning a fifth week of vacation Department heads proposed it, but the finance director said the vacation piece has a financial impact (payout liability on separation) that has not been budgeted. Some admin employees — including several in DPW — said this is not the right year given layoffs. At the April 27 hearing, the Select Board brought the article forward after a cost analysis. Officials said the cost is about $12,000, tied mainly to longevity; the three personal days carry no cost. FinCom voted to recommend adoption.

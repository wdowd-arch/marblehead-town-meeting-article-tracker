ARTICLE 15: Admin pay schedule

Quick facts
- Category: Compensation
- Sponsor: Select Board
- Vote threshold: Majority
- Key: 3 percent COLA effective July 1, 2026
- Stakes: low
- Recommendation: favorable (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Town meeting must vote each year to approve the pay schedules for non-union administrative employees — department heads, directors, coordinators and clerks across 11 pay grades. This year’s schedule applies a 3 percent cost-of-living increase effective July 1, 2026. The cost is already built into the departmental budgets. FinCom voted to recommend adoption with a 3 percent cost-of-living adjustment.

Primary source (warrant text)
ARTICLE 15: Proposed Reclassification and Pay Schedule (Administrative) 
To see if the Town will vote to amend Chapter 121 of the Bylaws, Classification and Wage Salary 
Plan, by changing certain job titles where indicated and reclassify certain positions in the 
Administrative Pay Schedule; to strike out the pay schedule as it relates to Administrative 
personnel, substitute in place thereof the new pay schedules and to transfer from available funds 
and/or appropriate a sum of money to make said new pay schedules effective, or take any other 
action relative thereto. Sponsored by the Select Board.

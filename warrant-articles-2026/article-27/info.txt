ARTICLE 27
Title: School Department Capital Needs
Sponsor: School Committee

Warrant (first lines):
- ARTICLE 27: School Department Capital Needs
- To see if the Town will vote to appropriate or transfer from available funds a sum of money for
- school department capital needs, and to raise the money for these purposes by the issue of bonds
- or notes, by the transfer of an unused/prior appropriation and borrowing authority for such
- purposes, by the passage of a one-time capital exclusion and/or debt exclusion or in any other
- manner, or take any other action relative thereto. Sponsored by the School Committee.

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Every year, some bills arrive after the town closes its books on June 30. Because Massachusetts law does not let municipalities pay prior-year invoices from the current budget, Town Meeting must appropriate the money separately — at a nine-tenths supermajority. This year’s batch covers seven departments, including Public Buildings, Rec & Park, Fire, Schools, the Assessor, Town Clerk and the Library.
- 
- A new purchase order system is expected to minimize future unpaid accounts. Historically the total has been higher than $60K.
- 
- At its April 27 hearing, FinCom re-voted the article after a late $36.98 school bill was added, with the updated appropriation reflected as $60,018.27. FinCom voted favorable on the revised number.

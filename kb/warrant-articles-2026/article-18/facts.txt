ARTICLE 18: Town officers pay

Quick facts
- Category: Compensation
- Sponsor: Select Board
- Vote threshold: Majority
- Key: Town clerk salary: $97,460
- Stakes: low
- Recommendation: favorable (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Under Marblehead’s bylaws, the town clerk is an elected officer whose salary must be set by Town Meeting. This article fixes her compensation at $97,460. All fees she collects (marriage licenses, certified copies, etc.) go to town revenue. She also receives longevity pay after five consecutive years of service. FinCom voted to recommend the yearly compensation.

Primary source (warrant text)
ARTICLE 18: Compensation – Town Officers 
To see if the Town will vote to revise the compensation of the Town Clerk as the Town by vote 
may determine and to transfer from available funds and/or appropriate a sum of money to make 
said revision effective, or take any other action relative thereto. Sponsored by the Select Board.

ARTICLE 39: Repeal Article 34, Department of Planning and Community Development

Quick facts
- Category: Citizen petition
- Sponsor: William Kuker and others
- Vote threshold: Majority
- Key: Established at 2024 Town Meeting
- Stakes: medium
- Recommendation: unfavorable (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Citizen petition to repeal the Department of Planning and Community Development, which was created by Article 34 at the 2024 annual Town Meeting. The department houses the town planner and oversees zoning, permitting and grant administration. It has secured $1.9M in grants and oversees $50M in active construction projects. Ironically, the department’s director presented a no-override budget that would eliminate his own position. FinCom Chair Goolsby noted this article may have financial implications because defunding the department would affect budgeted positions. At the April 27 hearing, FinCom discussed that the article is not advisory and would reverse the 2024 Town Meeting vote creating the department. It would eliminate and defund the department, including two currently funded planning positions. FinCom voted against recommending the article.

Primary source (warrant text)
ARTICLE 39: Repeal Article 34, Department of Planning and Community Development 
To repeal Article 34 as passed by 2024 Town Meeting establishing the “Department of Planning 
and Community Development” thereby eliminating, disbanding and de-funding said Department 
and all offices, positions and associations related to said Department. Or take any other action 
relative thereto. Sponsored by William Kuker and others.

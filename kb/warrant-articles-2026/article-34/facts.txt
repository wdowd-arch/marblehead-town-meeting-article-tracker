ARTICLE 34: Dissolve Public Works Committee

Quick facts
- Category: Governance
- Sponsor: Select Board
- Vote threshold: Majority
- Key: Competing with Article 35
- Stakes: medium
- Recommendation: no-action (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- The Public Works Committee was created by Town Meeting in 1970 but has been dormant for years. Town Administrator Kezer requested this article to formally dissolve it, arguing its advisory role is superseded by the DPW director position that now exists. If this passes, Article 35 (which would modernize the committee instead) becomes moot. The charter committee is also examining this issue. No financial implications; FinCom made no recommendation and said it would be a strong debate for Town Meeting floor.

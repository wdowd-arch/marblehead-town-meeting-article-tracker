ARTICLE 14: MWRA interest-free loan

Quick facts
- Category: Enterprise
- Sponsor: Water and Sewer Commission
- Vote threshold: Two-thirds
- Key: $3,500,000 interest-free
- Stakes: low
- Recommendation: favorable (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- The Massachusetts Water Resources Authority offers member communities interest-free loans for water infrastructure through its Local Financial Assistance Program. This article authorizes the town to borrow $3.5M at zero interest for construction and reconstruction of the water distribution system. The debt is repaid through water rates, not property taxes. Requires a two-thirds supermajority because it authorizes borrowing. FinCom voted to recommend the $3.5M borrowing authorization.

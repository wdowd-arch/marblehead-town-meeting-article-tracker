ARTICLE 38: Restore Elected Terms of the Select Board to One Calendar Year

Quick facts
- Category: Citizen petition
- Sponsor: William Kuker and others
- Vote threshold: Majority
- Key: Effective 2027 elections
- Stakes: medium
- Recommendation: pending (FinCom)
- Result: tbd

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Citizen petition that would reduce Select Board terms from three years to one year, effective with the 2027 municipal elections. This would mean all five seats are up for election every year instead of on a staggered cycle. Petitioner not present at the first FinCom hearing. FinCom deferred its recommendation to the second hearing (~April 27).

Primary source (warrant text)
ARTICLE 38: Restore Elected Terms of the Select Board to One Calendar Year 
To restore the elected terms of the Select Board to the duration of one calendar year, to take 
effect for the 2027 elections. Or take any other action relative thereto. Sponsored by William 
Kuker and others.

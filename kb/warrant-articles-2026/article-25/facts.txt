Quick facts
- Category: Capital

- Key: No requests this year

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Annual placeholder for capital improvements to school buildings. No requests were submitted for FY27, so it will be indefinitely postponed. If needs arise between now and Town Meeting, it could be revisited. FinCom made no recommendation since nothing is being requested.

Primary source (warrant text)
ARTICLE 25: School Buildings Capital Needs 
To see if the Town will vote to appropriate a sum of money for the purpose of making renovations 
and extraordinary repairs, including all professional feasibility studies, design, architectural and 
engineering fees, or significant capital expenditures for the Brown, Glover, Village, Veterans 
Middle, Marblehead High School and the school building known as the Eveleth School and their 
respective school grounds, and to raise the money for these purposes by the issue of bonds or 
notes, by the transfer of an unused/prior appropriation and borrowing authority for such 
purposes, by the passage of a one-time capital exclusion and/or debt exclusion or in any other 
manner, or take any other action relative thereto. Sponsored by the School Committee

the Stabilization Fund be a priority as funds are available. This is viewed favorably by bond rating

vote of Town Meeting.

Journalist notes (tracker)
- Placeholder article that appears on the warrant every year. It authorizes money for departments to buy or lease major equipment (vehicles, machinery, etc.). No department submitted a request this year, so it will be indefinitely postponed. FinCom made no recommendation since nothing is being requested.

Comment: The Fire Department’s budget included in the FY27 balanced budget includes the Town’s 
original estimate for Fire Department salaries. The Fire collective bargaining agreement was signed after 
the FY27 budget was finalized. The final agreement requires $143,238 of additional funds in excess of 
the estimate used in the balanced budget. Article 19 requests that amount be funded from available 
free cash.
